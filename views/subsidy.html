Ontario Electricity Support Program (OESP)

What is Ontario Electricity Support Program?

The OESP will reduce the cost of your household electricity by applying a monthly credit directly to your bill. The credit amount will depend on how many people live in your home and your combined household income. This is for lower income family, also the international student can also apply. If you new application Just Click and following the steps as below immediately!

What happens after you apply?

You will be notified of your eligibility for OESP approximately 4 to 6 weeks after we receive and review your completed application and signed consent forms. Upon approval, the credit will be applied directly to your electricity bill in approximately 6 to 8 weeks. If approved, you will receive OESP credits on your bill for a duration of 2 years before needing to reapply, or 5 years if you are aged 65+ or receiving a CPP disability pension.
