What we are Considered?

As international students ourselves, we understand firsthand the challenges and uncertainties that come with finding suitable housing in Canada for the first time. Driven by our own experiences and the desire to make this journey smoother for others like us, we decided to create a website. Our goal is to empower fellow international students with knowledge, resources, and support, enabling them to navigate the Canadian rental market with confidence and ease. We envision a platform where students can find not just a place to stay, but a home where they can thrive during their academic journey in this beautiful country.

Main Concepts

Rental Information

Rental information typically encompasses a wide range of details and guidelines relevant to the process of renting property. This information is vital for both landlords (property owners) and tenants (renters) as it helps them understand their rights, responsibilities, and the legalities involved in a rental agreement.

Canada Housing Benefit

In Canada, housing benefits include subsidies to help low-income families and individuals afford safe and suitable housing, reducing rent to a manageable portion of their income. Additionally, homeowners and renters may receive tax refunds or credits, such as the GST/HST New Housing Rebate and various provincial credits, to assist with housing costs and property taxes.

Traffic and Livelihood

Traffic and rental housing are two essential aspects that significantly impact student life. As students navigate their academic journey, they often encounter the challenges posed by traffic congestion and the search for suitable housing accommodations. The campus's location in a bustling urban area means that traffic patterns can affect commute times and accessibility to campus facilities.
